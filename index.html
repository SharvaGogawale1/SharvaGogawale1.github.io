Research

My goal is to develop AI systems that are not just technically powerful but socially aware, trustworthy, and aligned with human intent. Specifically, I am passionate about modeling social dynamics to evaluate LMs, addressing their performance disparities across language communities, and designing AI systems that drive meaningful social impact in high-stakes domains.
